Cats On My Block!

Pulaski Street, Bed-Stuy, Brooklyn

I have lived on Pulaski Street for almost three years. On my specific block, there is an empty lot filled with grass that many street cats call home!

These cats are mainly cared for by a woman on my block who has consistently given these cats food, water, and vet vists - likely for much longer than I've been on the block. With that being said, the names I have given the cats are of my own creation, for my own amusement.

This directory allows you to explore all my findings as I have gotten to know these feline friends =^-^=
